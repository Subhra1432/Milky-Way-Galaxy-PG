How the Token System Works

Our token system provides flexibility for residents who prefer not to commit to a full monthly plan. Purchase token packs in advance and use them whenever you need a meal.

  • Tokens can be purchased at the reception.
  • Present your token to the kitchen staff before meal times.
  • Tokens are valid for 3 months from the date of purchase.
  • One token is required for one meal.
